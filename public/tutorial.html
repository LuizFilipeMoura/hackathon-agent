Getting Started

The calculator allows you to perform basic mathematical operations between two numbers. Here's how to use it:

  1. Enter your first number in the first input field
  2. Enter your second number in the second input field
  3. Click on the operation button you want to perform
  4. The result will appear below the buttons

Available Operations

  • Add (+): Adds the two numbers together
  • Subtract (-): Subtracts the second number from the first
  • Multiply (×): Multiplies the two numbers
  • Divide (÷): Divides the first number by the second

Tips and Notes

Tip: You can use decimal numbers (e.g., 3.14) in your calculations.
Warning: When dividing, make sure your second number is not zero, as division by zero is not allowed.

Examples

  • To add 5 and 3: Enter 5 in first field, 3 in second field, click "Add" → Result: 8
  • To multiply 6 by 4: Enter 6 in first field, 4 in second field, click "Multiply" → Result: 24
  • To subtract 10 from 20: Enter 20 in first field, 10 in second field, click "Subtract" → Result: 10
  • To divide 15 by 3: Enter 15 in first field, 3 in second field, click "Divide" → Result: 5
