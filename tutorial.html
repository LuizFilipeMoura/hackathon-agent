Calculator Tutorial

Getting Started

Welcome to the Calculator tutorial! This guide will help you understand how to use our calculator effectively.

Basic Operations

  1. Enter the first number using the number pad
  2. Click on the operation you want to perform (+, -, ×, ÷)
  3. Enter the second number
  4. Press "=" to see your result
Tip: You can use the "C" button to clear your current calculation and start over.

Advanced Features

  • Use the decimal point for non-whole numbers
  • Chain multiple operations together
  • View your calculation history

Examples

Here are some example calculations:

  • Basic addition: 5 + 3 = 8
  • Multiplication: 6 × 4 = 24
  • Division with decimals: 10 ÷ 4 = 2.5
