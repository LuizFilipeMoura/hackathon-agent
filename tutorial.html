How to Use the Calculator

Getting Started

Our calculator is designed to be simple and intuitive. Here's how to get started:

  1. Enter your first number using the number pad
  2. Select the operation you want to perform (+, -, ×, ÷)
  3. Enter your second number
  4. Press the equals (=) button to see your result

Available Operations

  • Addition (+): Add two numbers together
  • Subtraction (-): Subtract the second number from the first
  • Multiplication (×): Multiply two numbers
  • Division (÷): Divide the first number by the second

Tips and Tricks

Tip #1: You can use the 'C' button to clear your current calculation and start over.
Tip #2: The calculator maintains the last result, so you can continue calculating with your previous answer.
Tip #3: Use the backspace button if you make a mistake while entering numbers.

Example Calculations

Here are some example calculations you can try:

  • Basic addition: 5 + 3 = 8
  • Multiple operations: 10 × 2 + 5 = 25
  • Working with decimals: 7.5 ÷ 2 = 3.75
